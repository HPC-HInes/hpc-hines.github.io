Our Park

  
  
    

About Hines Park

    

Hines Park is a natural treasure in our community. With miles of trails, scenic rivers, and open spaces, it is home to countless species of plants and wildlife as well as a place for families to gather, exercise, and enjoy nature.

    

Why We Care

    

The Conservancy is committed to keeping this park clean, safe, and accessible for everyone. By restoring habitats, improving trails, and organizing volunteer efforts, we help ensure that future generations will enjoy the same beauty we see today.
