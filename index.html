Who We Are

Protecting Hines Park through stewardship, partnership, and community action.

The Hines Park Conservancy is a nonprofit organization dedicated to preserving and strengthening one of our region’s most treasured public spaces.

We work to conserve, restore, and enhance the natural, cultural, and recreational resources of Hines Park, ensuring it remains a vibrant place for people and nature to thrive—today and for generations to come.

Through environmental stewardship, volunteerism, education, and community-driven projects, we support outdoor recreation, protect sensitive landscapes, and foster a deeper connection between people and the park.

We also partner closely with public agencies, community organizations, and local residents to improve and care for park lands and surrounding open spaces—always with the goal of serving the public good.
