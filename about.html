About Us

  
  
    

Our Mission

    

The Hines Park Conservancy’s mission is to protect, preserve, and restore Hines Park’s natural beauty while creating opportunities for recreation, community, and education.

    

Our Story

    

Founded in 2025, HPC brings together community members who care deeply about the future of Hines Park. Our volunteers, donors, and partners work hand-in-hand to make positive change possible.
