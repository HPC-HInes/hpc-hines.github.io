Our Projects

  
  
    

River Cleanup Days

    

Every month, volunteers come together to remove trash and debris from the riverbanks and trails. These cleanups protect wildlife and make the park more enjoyable for visitors.

    

Trail Restoration

    

We are restoring key trail sections to improve safety and accessibility. This includes resurfacing, signage, and planting native species.

    

Community Events

    

HPC organizes family-friendly events like nature walks, educational programs, and seasonal celebrations to bring the community together around the park.
